NETWORKING EVENTS

We have a jam-packed agenda in the Built Environment Networking Hub over the whole two days at London Build!

What better way to expand your connections than to visit all of our free-to-attend networking events to meet with thousands of attendees across the two days. Exchange business cards and connect with the UK construction industry's most influential figures. By registering for a free ticket to attend London Build, you'll gain access to all of the networking events at the show.  

Our full agenda of networking events at London Build will be released in May 2023. 

REGISTER YOUR INTEREST

OUR RECENT NETWORKING EVENTS INCLUDE

LONDON CONSTRUCTING EXCELLENCE CLUB BREAKFAST - “THE FUTURE IS DIGITAL; HOW DIGITAL IS CONSTRUCTION?”

This prestigious networking event will officially kick start the programme within the Built Environment Networking Hub. This complimentary breakfast will be held in collaboration with Constructing Excellence, a long-standing industry body comprised of regional centres and local business clubs that are actively improving industry performance from buildings to infrastructure, all parts of the demand and supply chain, all disciplines, professions and trades.

HOSTED BY:

FORUM FOR THE BUILT ENVIRONMENT NETWORKING

fbe is led by the industry for the industry. Their regional committees are all volunteers focused on linking likeminded people together to collaborate, debate and deliver excellence in the built environment. Join the Forum for the Built Environment for their National Networking Event.

HOSTED BY:

SUSTAINABILITY PROFESSIONALS NETWORKING 

HOSTED BY:

BIM OPEN MIC

HOSTED BY:

WOMEN IN BIM

WOMEN IN BIM is global online community with a vision to support, empower and celebrate female BIM and digital construction professionals. They believe that by working together and supporting women's career options and learning development opportunities they can help to retain a more diverse industry.

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH:

ARCHITECTURE SOCIAL AWARDS

Grab a cheeky beer and join Stephen Drew - Founder of Architecture Social for Architecture Social's "Surely this can't be a proper Award Ceremony?!" Award Ceremony, celebrating the talent, dedication and passion of the UK's Architecture industry.

HOSTED BY:

URBANO BUILD NETWORKING BREAKFAST

With many firms short of skilled staff, finding ways for larger companies to fully utilise the experience and specialisms of the largest workforce in the UK – SMES – remains for many on both sides of the procurement table a tough challenge. This interactive event will explore what needs to be done to lower and remove the barriers to supply chain opportunities for small and micro businesses, with time for participants to develop new contacts after the speaker discussion has ended.

HOSTED BY:

NETWORKING LUNCH: HOW TO BUILD BETTER CLIENT EXPERIENCES

Hear from Bruntwood on how they are leading the way in building vibrant communities by delivering outstanding office, co-working, retail and lab spaces - with employee wellbeing and the client experience as top of mind. Learn how values, employee experience, and sustainability can all play a part in delighting the client and growing your business.
We will give you an opportunity to exchange thoughts on or even debate what a better client relationship looks like over the lunch provided and some beverages. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn tips from your peers and by digitally transforming your business you can attract, understand, nurture, and engage clients to evolve experience and deepen trust.

HOSTED BY:

MENTAL HEALTH IN CONSTRUCTION NETWORKING

London Build will also be working with an amazing team of Mental Health in Construction Ambassadors, who are committed to raising awareness and challenging the stigma around Mental Health. Join our Ambassadors at the networking event to hear what they're doing to empower and support their peers. 

HOSTED BY:

HOUZZ HAPPY HOUR

Join the Houzz Pro team to learn how the software could take your business to the next level, mingle with other builders and have a drink on the Houzz.

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH: 

THE UK'S LARGEST NETWORKING EVENT FOR WOMEN IN CONSTRUCTION

The annual Women in Construction Networking Event, in partnership with NAWIC, is free-to-attend for all. Learn from an inspiring panel of industry leaders as they celebrate the successes and discuss the challenges facing female AEC professionals. Network with fellow professionals and feel inspired by the diverse stories that make up one of the most exciting industries. 

SPONSORED BY: 

ARCHITECTURE SOCIAL NETWORKING 

Dust off that pile of business cards you printed before the pandemic and join Architecture Social in an informal networking session!

HOSTED BY:

DIVERSITY IN CONSTRUCTION NETWORKING EVENT

The annual Diversity in Construction Networking Event in partnership with Building Equality, Constructability, Let's Build and Building People is free-to-attend for all. Learn from an inspiring panel of industry leaders as they celebrate the successes and discuss the challenges facing BAME, LGBT+ and female AEC professionals. Network with fellow professionals and feel inspired by the diverse stories that make up one of the most exciting industries.

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH:

CIOB HUB IN THE PUB NETWORKING DRINKS

Join the CIOB London & Hemel Hub Committees and Tomorrow’s Leaders group for their laid-back social gathering, Hub in the Pub. Join our members to chat about what we've been up to locally, and how you can get involved. CIOB members and non-members are welcome to join us, just be sure to bring a business card and exchange it for your drink voucher.
