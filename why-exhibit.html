WHY EXHIBIT?

London Build is an award-winning show and is widely viewed as one of the best construction exhibitions in the world.

Winner of Best Brand Expansion at the Exhibition News Awards and finalist of Best Trade Show in the UK at the AEO Awards. 

The feedback from all exhibitors and attendees really speaks for itself. Attracting thousands of high-quality attendees from across the UK’s entire construction industry, this is your one-stop show to network with key buyers, learn of the latest project opportunities and acquire thousands of new business leads.
