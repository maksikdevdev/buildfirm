ABOUT LONDON BUILD

The UK's biggest festival of construction features a jam-packed two days of incredible content with 500+ inspiring speakers across 8 stages, hundreds of exhibitors, entertainment, celebrity guests, networking parties, live music and much more!

FIND OUT MORE

WHY EXHIBIT AT LONDON BUILD?

The feedback from all exhibitors and attendees really speaks for itself. Attracting thousands of high-quality attendees from across the UK’s entire construction industry, this is your one-stop show to network with key buyers, learn of the latest project opportunities and acquire thousands of new business leads.

FIND OUT MORE

LONDON CONSTRUCTION AWARDS

The London Construction Awards celebrate the achievements, developments and innovation across London’s construction industry. Featuring a wide range of categories for suppliers, contractors, architects, civil engineers, developers, housebuilders, local authorities and more.

ENTER THE AWARDS

EXCLUSIVE NETWORKING EVENTS

What better way to expand your connections than to visit all of our free-to-attend networking events to meet with thousands of attendees across the two days. Exchange business cards and connect with the UK construction industry's most influential figures. 

GET INVOLVED

WOMEN IN CONSTRUCTION

London Build hosts the world's largest networking event of Women in Construction. The session is free-to-attend for all women (and men!). Come along to network, learn and be inspired. Join experts discussing the untapped opportunities for women working in construction.

FIND OUT MORE

DIVERSITY & INCLUSION IN CONSTRUCTION

London Build works with a large team of champions driving change, diversity & inclusion in the construction industry. This annual networking event is free-to-attend (everyone is welcome!) and offers the chance to network, learn from diversity champions, discuss key issues and feel inspired!
