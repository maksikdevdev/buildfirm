CONSTRUCTION IDOLS

The Construction Idols shortlist celebrates the most influential & inspiring professionals working in London's built environment.

We want to celebrate and share the significant contributions that these leaders have made to the AEC industries. They could be:

  • Architects that have designed iconic landmarks or the latest projects
  • People that fight for the rights and safety of workers through their work with associations
  • Mentors that create programs and opportunities to build the future construction workforce
  • Long-serving leaders of the top firms 
  • Sustainability heroes changing the landscape of the built environment 
  • Innovators that are pioneering new technologies 
  • People that have overcome barriers to achieve success and serve as an inspiration to others
  • And many more!
