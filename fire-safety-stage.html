The Fire Safety Stage attracts thousands of fire safety professionals from across the UK to learn from leading industry experts on passive fire protection, fire detection and prevention.

Gain access to hundreds of high-level speakers across the two days, plus 100+ hours of CPD presentations, interactive panel discussions and training workshops with leading fire safety experts from contractors, civil engineers, government and more.
