The Architecture Stage attracts thousands of AEC professionals from across the UK to learn of the latest developments, innovations and project opportunities the industry has to offer. 

Gain access to hundreds of high-level speakers across the two days, plus 100+ hours of CPD presentations, interactive panel discussions and training workshops with leading architects, architectural technologists, specifiers, contractors, civil engineers, developers and more.
